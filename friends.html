Making Friends:

Having friends is always fun!



Here are some good things to look for in a friend:

  1. A good friend listens to you.



  2. A good friend is nice to you.



  3. A good friend helps you.



  4. A good friend protects you from bullies.



  5. A good friend plays together with you.



If you find a friend that does all of these things, you found a good friend.
