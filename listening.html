Listening At School:

At school, when the teacher is talking, I need to listen to the teacher.




To listen to the teacher, I need to not talk while he or she is talking.



When I listen to the teacher, I learn new things.



If I listen to the teacher, the teacher will be happy and want to help me.



If I don't listen to the teacher, the teacher will be mad.
