Check out this activity where you drag and drop the letters to make words. It's called The Claw.



Here are two different levels and their descriptions.

  • Level One: three letter words with basic individual sounds (consonant, vowel, consonant)


  • Level Two: three and four letter words with basic individual sounds with consonants next to each other (vowel, consonant, consonant),(consonant, vowel, consonant, consonant), etc
